Learn About Us

We are a non profit organisation principally engaged in charity and team building activities

Tyche is a non profit organization an it is run and owned by students at tertiary level of education . It works like any other financial institution yet its principality engages in building exercises, charity and building a superior tomorrow. .
To make positive impact on the society and to work towards creating a better world for all.
Creating a better everyday life for many people and building a superior tomorrow.
