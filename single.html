Membership

A qualified individual is eligible to apply for mrmbership with Tyche by filling an application form and payment of a registration fee of KES 100.

Subscription fees, Registered members approved by the board of directors are required to pay a monthly subscription fee of KES 100 to ensure the company's operations are running well. Payments are done via our corporate Treasurer, Mr. Samuel Muhatia. Payment details include Safaricom MPESA number 0708936070(SAMUEL MUHATIA), Updated payment details will be posted later.

Shares and Share holders

To ensure there is a steady cash flow into the company, and to avoid overdependence on donors and subscription fee-our team conducted a research on safer investment methods. The company has a maximum of 10,000 shares, details of becoming an investor in Tyche will be drafted in our constitution.

LOANS

Tyche members are eigible for a loan after three months of active membership, inquries about loans shall be submitted to the Loans and recovery officer.

How to seek for HELP

Members of Tyche can seek for help of any kind by contacting our social and public relations officer, Sir Johnson Mango through his email address. johnsonmango332@gmail.com. Members in suspension due to gross misconduct are not eligible to apply for any kind of assistance until the suspension period is over.

FUN ZONE

Tyche will be organizing for recreational activities twice a year for it's members. Members will showcase their talents and win prices, an artist wil be invited to these events to promote music and art.
